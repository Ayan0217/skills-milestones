Self-Literacy​

Developing the ability for Self-Literacy.

Let's take 5 minutes to discuss how to acquire Self-Literacy skills.

Self-Literacy is the process of acquiring knowledge, skills, and understanding on one's own initiative, without formal guidance from a teacher or structured curriculum, when you set learning goals based on your own interests.

Self-literacy is a journey of self-discovery, a lifelong learning process that brings unparalleled rewards. It emphasizes the importance of knowing oneself, which in turn makes one excel in everything they do. Self-literacy is seen as an important gift that can be given to anyone, setting them on the path to understanding themselves and their place in the world.
